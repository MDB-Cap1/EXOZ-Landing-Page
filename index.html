AI-Enhanced

at Commercial Scale

eXoZymes is the market leader of next-generation biomanufacturing, making highly valuable, natural-inspired products and molecules, in quality, purity, and at a scale, that no one else can. eXoZymes uses AI-enhanced, cell-free enzyme technology to produce unique products. The core idea is simple but powerful: nature makes extraordinary molecules — but only in trace amounts, locked inside complex biological systems of cells. eXoZymes figured out how to replicate those same molecules at industrial scale, with engineering-level precision, by working entirely outside the living cell. The result is a scalable platform with durable IP, capital-light growth, and asymmetric upside across pharmaceutical, wellness and industrial markets.

Learn more from eXoZymes CEO Michael Heltzen and Chief Commercial Officer Damien Perriman on upcoming Investor Zoom calls as they walk you through the platform and flagship product programs — each targeting hundred-billion-dollar-plus total addressable markets — and why the timing for eXoZymes is right now.
